ffs signs archive

Ffs sign archive collects a series of photographs taken at the chiasso freight railway yard. The images were taken at two different times, initially in 2002/2003 with an analogue camera and later in 2022 with a digital reflex. The aim of this archive is to investigate the shells of freight cars, which due to a series of events (wear, manipulation, graffiti) take on an unpredictable and interesting aesthetic value. The navigation of this archive is mainly managed by machine learning (handpose).
